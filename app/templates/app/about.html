📘 About the Student Leave System

The Student Leave Application System is a streamlined platform designed to simplify the process of submitting, reviewing, and managing student leave requests. Whether it's for medical reasons, family emergencies, or personal matters, students can quickly submit their leave requests online without the need for physical paperwork.

🎯 Key Features:

  • 📝 Simple and intuitive leave request form
  • 📅 Customizable leave start and end dates
  • 🗂️ Faculty & semester-wise tracking
  • 🔒 Soft delete with Recycle Bin support
  • 📧 Email and contact record keeping
  • 🔍 Easy data viewing, editing, and filtering

💡 Why Use This System?

This system eliminates delays, reduces paper usage, and provides a centralized dashboard for both students and administrators to manage leave efficiently. It supports academic institutions in tracking attendance-related data and ensures transparent communication between students and the faculty.

👨‍💻 Developed By:

This system was built using Django and Bootstrap with a focus on simplicity, accessibility, and performance. Designed for educational institutions that value time, structure, and clarity.
